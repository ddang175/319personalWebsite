Cyphonic

Cyphonic is an advanced web application developed to help Iowa State University students connect based on their music preferences, leveraging data from the Spotify API. The platform provides an in-depth look at users' listening habits, showcasing detailed stats on their favorite artists, songs, genres, and more. With a focus on presenting users with an analysis of their music taste, Cyphonic offers both an entertaining and insightful experience by giving users a better understanding of how unique or “basic” their music preferences are. At its core, Cyphonic utilizes the Spotify API to gather and display comprehensive data about a user’s listening history. Once logged in with their Spotify account, users can instantly see a breakdown of their most-played tracks, favorite artists, and favorite genres. Through this data, Cyphonic not only showcases user statistics but also helps users discover new music through detailed insights on their listening trends.
